Canarys Launches Auryis: AI-Powered Pharma Compliance

Canarys Automations Limited has recently advanced its innovation portfolio with the launch of Auryis, an AI-powered compliance platform specifically tailored for the Pharma and Life Sciences sector.

Leveraging cutting-edge Large Language Models (LLMs) and custom machine learning algorithms, Auryis streamlines regulatory alignment, automated audit prep, and risk detection across global development operations.
